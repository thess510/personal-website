Personal Interests



Movies

I love to go to the movie theater and watch movies, especially with my friends. I have loved movies since my younger days when I wanted to be an actor. I even made my own short films! Making those films was a great memory and makes me appreciate the talent and hard work required to make a quality movie.

Photography

I recently developed an interest in photography. I take great joy in stopping in a scenic spot and trying to produce a great photograph. I have come to enjoy the challenge of finding a great subject for a photograph and then producing a beautiful image. This hobby also makes me stop and appreciate the scenery around me.
