About me



Bio

Hello, I am Tanner Hess. Thanks for visiting my personal website. I am a Computer Information Technology (CIT) student at Northern Kentucky University (NKU), concentrating on Cybersecurity. My hometown is Elizabethtown, Kentucky, and I graduated from Central Hardin High School in Cecilia, Kentucky during the COVID-19 year of 2020.

During my senior year of high school, I took my first IT classes, and I have had an interest in CIT as a major since that time. I participated in the 17 week Greater Knox Coding Academy during my last semester of high school, where I spent 14 weeks studying Java and 3 weeks studying Security +. Although I did not receive any certifications, the experience was rewarding. I planned on attending NKU for my freshman year of college. However, due to COVID-19, I decided to attend Elizabethtown Community and Technical College for my freshman year. I am excited to be at NKU and to be studying Cybersecurity.
