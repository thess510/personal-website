About me



Bio

Tanner is a Computer Information Technology (CIT) major studying at Northern Kentucky University (NKU). He is also on the track to specialize in Cybersecurity. He is from Elizabethtown, KY and attended Central Hardin High School in Cecilia, KY.

During his Senior year of high school is when he started taking his first IT related classes, and has had an interest ever since. Tanner planned on attending NKU for his freshman year of college, however, the impact COVID-19 would have had on his college experience would have cost him more money than what it normally would have been. Therefore, he attended Elizabethtown Community and Technical College for his freshman year. He took mostly gen eds with the exception of a couple CIT classes so that when he was able to attend NKU the next year, he could focus mainly on his major classes.
